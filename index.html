About the project

This digitization project was created for the course Digitizing Cultural Hertiage at Borås University. This collection consists of 18 botanical illustrations from a german textbook about botanical bilology.

The book is called Underricht in der Botanik by Dr. Otto Vogel, Dr. Karl Müllenhoff abd Dr. Paul Röseler. It was orginally published in in Berlin 1906 by Winckelmann & Shöne. The 18 illustrations chosen for this collection could be found in the back of the book. They are threcolor-prints of watercolors painted by an artist called A. Schmalfuís.

The book pages were gently removed from the book and scanned using 600dpi, and then cropped and saved as tif-files.

All tif-files of the original scans have been uploaded on Google Drive and can be accessed here.

Project work by Anna Daghall for the course Digitizing Cultueral Heritage 2022
